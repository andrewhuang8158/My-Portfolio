About Me

I've always liked hands on activities and creating things myself. From playing with Legos to woodworking and now creating with code, the infinite possibility and design limited only by imagination always enraptured me. I hope to one day create or be a part of something that revolutionizes the world and I think web development is a great place to start. To start my career in the computer sciences and software development, I dabbled in free online sources in coding some of which were The Odin Project and Harvard's CS50. Fascinated with the things I've learned I decided to take a bootcamp with a few friends While there, I enjoyed collaborating with peers, sharing progress and achievements with groupmates, and most of all, overcoming obstacles. I can now confidently and autonomously code in the frontend and backend. In my free time, I like to go out, explore new places, hike, run, gym, etc. Since I like to try new things, I believe that I have a natural affinity for coding as a lot of it involves new technologies, techniques, and trials and errors. I believe my love for learning and progressing in all avenues of life make me the ideal candidate for software engineering as I continue to tinker and adjust on the fly.
